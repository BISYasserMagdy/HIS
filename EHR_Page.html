⚙️ How the EHR Workflow Works

From patient registration to discharge — every step is captured and accessible.

Patient Registration

Demographics, insurance, and consent forms captured digitally at check-in.

Triage & Vitals

Nursing staff record vitals directly into the chart; abnormals are flagged instantly.

Provider Encounter

Physicians document visit notes using templates, link diagnoses with ICD-10 codes.

Orders & Prescriptions

Lab, imaging, and medication orders sent electronically to the respective departments.

Results & Review

Results auto-import into the record; providers are notified for critical values.

Billing & Discharge

Encounter is coded, claims generated, and the patient's record is complete for next visit.
